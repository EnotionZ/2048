Instructions:

  • Start game by clicking on screen or pushing spacebar
  • Keep doing the above motion to avoid the pipes
  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move tiles
  • Score is based on tile movement, not pipe clearing
  • You have 3 lives which decrements when colliding with pipe or hitting the ground
